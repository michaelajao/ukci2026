\subsection{Forecasting Accuracy (E1)}\label{ssec:e1}

\Cref{tab:forecast_accuracy} reports root mean squared error (RMSE) by
horizon for the proposed PINN-GRU with decision-aware loss against
statistical, machine-learning, and recurrent baselines on
mechanical-ventilation bed occupancy. The proposed model trades a small
amount of root-mean-squared accuracy for a substantially lower
underestimation rate, consistent with the asymmetric design of
$\mathcal{L}_{\text{under}}$ in \Cref{eq:under_loss}.

\subsection{Regional Allocation (E2)}\label{ssec:e2}

to allocation baselines on the seven-region NHS England case study, fixing
the medium budget level and 120-minute travel-time threshold. The robust
MILP achieves the lowest worst-case shortage, while deterministic MILP and
demand-proportional allocation tie on expected unmet demand under the median
forecast.
